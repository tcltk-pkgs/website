About

TclTkpkgs is a web interface to search for Tcl/Tk packages and libraries.

Why does tcltkpkgs exist?

Mainly for fun, to explore the Tcl/Tk ecosystem with modern web technologies.

How do I add my package?

Submit a PR to the registry with your package information.

Disclaimer on Metadata Accuracy :

Please note that despite best efforts, some packages may have incorrect license or author information due to the automated nature of data collection. I apologize for any inaccuracies this may have caused.

If you spot an error in attribution or licensing for your project, please contact me so I can rectify it immediately. Your contributions to the Tcl/Tk ecosystem are valued and deserve proper recognition.
